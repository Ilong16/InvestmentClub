Welcome to the Seattle Academy Investment Club

Welcome! We meet 1-3 times a month in AC106 and over the course of the year we will be hosting guest speakers. If you are interested in signing up, contact Isaac Long at: isaaclong@seattleacademy.org

View DemoLearn more
Lessons

SAIC starts the year with lessons about the stock market and investment opporitunities. This includes key concepts and general guidelines to get you started.

MarketWatch Year-long League

Throughout the year we will be using MarketWatch in order to keep engaged in the stock market and the global economy.

Guest Speakers

Throughout the year there will be guest speakers who work at JP Morgan and other financial firms from around the Seattle area come in to talk to the class.

Outside Activities

Throughout the year there will be a few opporituinites to go to firms to get a feel for the world of investing and financial firms.
